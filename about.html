About the Clinic

One Team. One Location. Every Case.

No franchise, no rotating strangers. The clinicians who evaluate you are the ones who treat you and follow you through your full growth cycle.

Our Story

Miami Hair Transplant was built around a simple idea: hair restoration is personal, and it should not feel like a factory. Too many patients are handed between technicians they never met, sold a package, and sent home. We do the opposite.

Every case runs under the supervision of our Medical Director, from the first scalp evaluation to the final follow-up. We design your hairline with you, we tell you honestly when a procedure is not the right move, and we stay with you through the twelve months it takes for a transplant to fully mature. That is the standard, on every case.
