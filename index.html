Our Advanced Procedures

Precision and Innovation
in Hair Restoration

FUE Hair Transplant

We move your own follicles, one at a time, from the back of your head to the thinning areas. No linear scar.

Learn more →

PRP Treatment

We spin down your own blood and inject the platelet-rich plasma to slow shedding and thicken what's there. Often paired with a transplant.

Learn more →

Hairline Restoration

We design a new hairline to fit your face, then rebuild it with a soft, natural front edge.

Learn more →

Eyebrow Transplant

We rebuild thin or over-plucked brows hair by hair, set at the angle real brow hair grows.

Learn more →

Beard Transplant

We fill patchy cheeks, the jawline, and scars where hair won't grow on its own, using your own follicles.
