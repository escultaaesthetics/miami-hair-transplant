Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Miami Hair Transplant (“we,” “us,” or “the clinic”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you submit a consultation request or contact form, we collect the information you provide, which may include your name, phone number, email address, photographs you choose to share, and details about your hair-restoration goals. We also collect basic technical data (such as browser type and pages visited) through standard website analytics.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your consultation request and contact you about treatment options
  • To schedule appointments and provide care
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send you information you have requested

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate the clinic and this website (for example, our appointment and customer-management platform), and only as needed to provide our services or as required by law.

Photographs

Photographs you submit for evaluation are used only to assess your candidacy and plan your care. We never publish patient photographs without separate, explicit written consent.

Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you by contacting us at info@miahairtransplant.com. You may also opt out of marketing messages at any time.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to info@miahairtransplant.com or (786) 952-6821, or by mail to 860 NW LeJeune Rd, Suite 402, Miami, Florida 33126.

This policy is provided for general information and should be reviewed by legal counsel before publication to ensure it meets HIPAA, Florida, and applicable federal requirements for your practice.
